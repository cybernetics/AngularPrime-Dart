Tetris on AngularDart

This is an early prototype of Tetris running on AngularPrime/Dart.
Currently, I've tested AngularTetris with Google Chrome 35, Firefox 30 and Internet Explorer 11. It may or may not work with other browsers. Please note the Javascript version starts a lot slower than the Dartium version.
